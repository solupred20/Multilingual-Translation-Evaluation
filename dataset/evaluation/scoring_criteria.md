# Translation Evaluation Criteria

## Accuracy
- Is the meaning correctly transferred from source to target?

## Fluency
- Is the translation natural and easy to read?

## Terminology
- Are domain-specific terms (e.g., medical terms) correctly translated?

## Cultural Adaptation
- Is the translation appropriate for the target audience?

## Dialect Handling
- Does the translation correctly reflect the required dialect (e.g., Saudi Arabic vs Modern Standard Arabic)?
