# Saudi Dialect & Transcription Examples

## Example 1

Audio (simulated):
"وش اخبارك اليوم؟"

Transcription:
وش اخبارك اليوم؟

Standard Arabic:
كيف حالك اليوم؟

English:
How are you today?

---

## Example 2

Audio (simulated):
"خذ الدواء بعد الاكل"

Transcription:
خذ الدواء بعد الاكل

Standard Arabic:
تناول الدواء بعد الطعام

English:
Take the medication after food

---

## Example 3

Audio (simulated):
"حسيت بدوخة بعد الدواء"

Transcription:
حسيت بدوخة بعد الدواء

Standard Arabic:
شعرت بدوخة بعد تناول الدواء

English:
I felt dizzy after taking the medication

---

## Notes

- Saudi dialect uses informal structures and simplified grammar
- Transcription must reflect spoken language, not formal Arabic
- Context is critical for accurate translation and evaluation
