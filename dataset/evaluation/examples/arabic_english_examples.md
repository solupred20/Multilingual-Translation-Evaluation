# Arabic-English Translation Evaluation Examples

## Example 1

Source:
The patient should take the medication daily.

Translation A:
يجب على المريض تناول الدواء يومياً.

Translation B:
المريض ياخذ الدواء كل يوم.

Evaluation:
- A: More formal and medically appropriate ✔
- B: Informal (dialect), less suitable in medical context

Final Ranking:
A > B


---

## Example 2

Source:
This drug may cause dizziness.

Translation A:
هذا الدواء قد يسبب دوخة.

Translation B:
الدواء ده ممكن يدوخك.

Evaluation:
- A: Accurate and formal ✔
- B: Informal Egyptian dialect

Final Ranking:
A > B


---

## Example 3 (Saudi Dialect)

Source:
How are you?

Translation A:
كيف حالك؟

Translation B:
وش اخبارك؟

Evaluation:
- A: Standard Arabic
- B: Saudi dialect ✔

Final Ranking:
Depends on context (formal vs informal)
